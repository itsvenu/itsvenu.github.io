Know me

Hallo, I’m a bioinformatics PhD student working on [epi]genomics and other genome wide high throughput data. Though I work with data in many different contexts, my main interest is understanding tumor promoting mechanisms of pediatric cancers.

In addition to working on cancer related data, I’m highly interested in the field of machine learning & data science to generate hypotheses, answer questions and explore data to unravel the hidden patterns by building models.

I would love to simplify scientific findings to spread the knowledge to a broader audience. I believe general public needs to understand the scientific work in a meaningful way instead of imbibing wrong information from random websites on the internet.

And programming is love!
